## 🛠️ How to Manage Content

### 📢 Posting Notices
1. Log in to the Admin Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Notice Board**.
3. Click **New Announcement**.
4. Fill in the title, category, and content. You can also upload a **PDF circular** or an **Image**.
5. Click **Post Announcement**. It will appear instantly on the home page and notice board.

### 🖼️ Updating Gallery
1. Navigate to **Gallery Manager**.
2. Click **Upload New Image**.
3. Add a caption and select a category (Campus, Labs, Events).
4. The image will be auto-optimized and added to the live gallery.

### 🎓 Viewing Admissions
1. Navigate to **Admissions**.
2. Here you can see a list of all students who have submitted interest forms online.
3. You can **export the list to Excel** for offline processing.
